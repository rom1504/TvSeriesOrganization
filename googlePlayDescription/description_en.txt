Organize all your Tv Series with Tv Series Organizer
- List of episodes organized by season and series
- From http://www.thetvdb.com/ database, download list of episode and season with banners, poster, description
- Good looking interface
- Track the episode and series you have seen
- Air time of episodes
- Display upcoming series/season/episode
- Search and add any series you want

This is an open source project, if you want more information or want to contribute go to https://github.com/rom1504/TvSeriesOrganizer
